Incident Media

Quotes

At approximately 1am on October 22nd 2023, 12 civilians, including at least 11 members of the Mansour family, were reportedly killed when an alleged Israeli bombardment hit their home on Aed Al Bashiti Street, in the Al Geneina neighbourhood of Rafah. An unspecified number of others were wounded. A thirteenth member of the Mansour family was also killed, and was a militant affiliated with Palestinian Islamic Jihad (PIJ). On March 20, 2026, PIJ published a non-exhaustive list of commanders killed in Gaza since October 7, 2023. 28-year-old Abdullah Daniel Mansour was among those named and photographed. As such, he has been recorded as a militant. 53-year-old Nimat Muhammad Mansour (Bawadi, wife of Ghaleb Kamel Mansour) was reportedly killed in the attack, alongside her two children 25-year-old Mahmoud Ghaleb Mansour and 17-year-old Fatima Ghaleb Mansour. Nimat’s sister, 47-year-old Khitam Muhammad Mansour Bawadi was also killed in the attack, along with four of her children, 15-year-old Alaa Daniel Mansour, 20-year-old Samer Daniel Kamel Mansour, 25-year-old Bisan Daniel Mansour and 28-year-old Abdullah Daniel Mansour. Abdullah’s wife, 28-year-old Doaa Moeen Bawadi, died in the attack as did their two young daughters 3-year-old Jordi Abdullah Mansour and 6-year-old Dania Abdullah Mansour, and their son, 1-year-old Daniel Abdullah Mansour. A girl named Maria Khaled Zakaria Al-Shanti who was less than a year old was also killed. Sources reported that Doaa was pregnant at the time she was killed.
