Summary / Notes

Two days after the start of the last truce (Jan 2025), a member of the Al-Dabaka family shared a detailed post mourning numerous relatives killed in the war. Among them was Al Quds TV photojournalist Hossam Manal Al-Dabbaka, referred to as “the mujahid commander.” The post was concluded with a prayer for God to accept their jihad.
