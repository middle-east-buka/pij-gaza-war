Summary / Notes

On 17 October 2023, an explosion occurred at Al-Ahli Arab Hospital in Gaza. Based on several independent investigations and intelligence assessments, the incident is most consistent with a misfired Palestinian rocket that failed shortly after launch and impacted the hospital grounds, rather than an air-delivered strike. The casualties are therefore classified as Non-Hostile (War-Related) — Internal Fire / Munition Misfire. Casualty data source: The casualty list for this incident was taken from Airwars casualty analysis.
