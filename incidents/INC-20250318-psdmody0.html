At dawn on March 18th, 2025, a reported Israeli airstrike targeted a residential structure in the Nuseirat refugee camp, located in the central Gaza Strip. The strike resulted in the deaths of multiple members of the Abu Seif family. 25-year-old Shaimaa Mahmoud Ibrahim Wshah was killed, as was her brother-in-law, 26-year-old Ghassan Mahir Motar Abu Seif. Ghassan’s wife, Sarah Abd al-Rahman Omar Sabah (Abu Seif) was killed with her son, 11-month-old Seif Ghassan Maher Abu Seif. Ghassan’s brother, Dima Maher Abu Seif, was also reported killed.
